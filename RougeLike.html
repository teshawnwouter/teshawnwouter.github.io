Game discription

Brave hero who figths all the monster the world throws at him overwelming him he must fight till his last breath.

My role

This was a rougelike -lite idea where i was also trying new things such as getter and setter. I had a plan on making a 2D combat systeem which worked really well. Also working with poly colliders and making costum ones to fit the attack swing of the character. There are a few enemys were most are the same but all have different stats and can be ajsuted on the fly. All enemies have their own attacks and own damage. If more time was given i would give the enemies more than a single attack.

Learning process

This was a game which pushed my knowledge of scriptableObjects to the max. I had to use them in the project and wanted to make sure they were for the powerUps/magic. I have also learned how Game design and and technical design work. This was the first time i had made one and got use to it soon after. The power Ups also have random drop chance and either spawns in or doesnt.

Things i would improve

If i had more time i would make more levels and double down on the comabt systeem. I would make delay combos and would make status effects for the spells.
