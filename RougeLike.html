Game discription

Brave hero who figths all the monster the world throws at him overwelming him he must fight till his last breath.

My role

This was a roguelike-lite idea. Where I was also trying new things such as getters and setters. I had a plan on making a 2D Combat system, which worked really well. Also, working with poly colliders and making costumes. Ones to fit the attack swing of the character. There are a few enemies, and most are the same. But all have different stats and can be adjusted on the fly. All enemies have their own Attacks and own damage. If more time was given, I would give the enemies more than a single Attack.

Learning process

This was a game that pushed my knowledge of scriptable objects to the max. I had To use them in the project and wanted to make sure they were for power-ups/magic. I also have Learned how game design and technical design work. This was the first time I had made one. And got used to it soon after. The power-ups also have a random drop chance and either spawn in or Doesn't.

Things i would improve

If I had more time, I would make more levels and double down on the combat. System. I would make delay combos and would make status effects for the spells.
