My role

My pride and joy (for now). This is a solo project. That took inspiration from Only Up and Getting Over It. It's a difficult platform where Most of my skills came from and are shown. I did wallrunning, grappling, and swinging as well as Basic movement. There isn't really anything I would change about this other than making the Level longer. The model was made by me and rigged by me. It was the first time I had Use a blender. This scored almost perfect in the exam it was for. It's also where I found the The style of coding works the best for me.

Learning process

In this project I have learned how making a contract works and how it works. Best for me as a coder. I learned to be efficient with my work and how to get stuff done asap. There are not many bad things I can say about the things I have learned to do on this project. It holds a place in my heart.

Things I would improve

If I had more room and time, I would make the level longer. I would also Implement online play and player interactions where you can pull each other to yourself. There would also be voice communication.
