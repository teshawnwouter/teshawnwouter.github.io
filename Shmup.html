Game discription

Go through space and seek and rank up score see how many you can destroy. Use uniqe powers in your arsenal

My role

This game was my second shmup i made i was getting the hang of using scriptableObjects and trying new things like TimeSlow which reduses the Time Scale of the game. I also worked on a systeem that has perminant Upgrades and temporary powerUps. It was an over all blast to make and i was thrilled with the end result. I would have loved to add more to the Game but ultimately ran out of time. I had also made a blackHole which pulls in enemies and renderes them unable to get out till the ability is gone.

Learning process

My second shmup and the first game where i started my hunt for knowledge. I this game is where i learned what i wanted to do as a developer i know here that making good gameplay was my dream. I manage to make a time slow, shield, and pull shot. Not much bad to say about this project it was the start of my gameplay development days.

Things i would improve

I would love to add a high score and make the game more chaotic. The gameplay would be nice to improve upon as awell but it was the early days.
